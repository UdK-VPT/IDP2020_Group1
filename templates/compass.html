Step 1 : Scan the QR code using your smartphone. You will be redirected to https://lamplightdev.github.io/compass/.
Step 2 : Use the compass on the website to find the angle between the wall along "LENGTH" and the closest pole.
EXPLANATION : If you are in the northern hemisphere, you should provide as input the angle between the wall and north pole. If you are in the southern hemisphere, you should provide as input the angle between the wall and south pole.
NOTE : Most smartphones have a pre-installed compass application. You are free to use that application as well.
